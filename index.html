LONDON

London is the capital and largest city of England and the United Kingdom, located in the southeast of England on the River Thames. It is a major global center for finance, culture, and history, with landmarks like the Houses of Parliament, Buckingham Palace, and the Tower of London.

PARIS

Paris is the capital and most populous city of France, located in the north-central part of the country on the Seine River. It is a global center for art, fashion, culture, and business, famous for landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum, and is widely considered a major tourist destination. The city is politically divided into 20 administrative districts known as arrondissements.

Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital and largest city of Japan, a global metropolis that blends ultramodern skyscrapers and technology with historic temples and traditional culture. It is an economic and political center, known for its fashion, food, and vibrant urban districts, and is situated on the Pacific coast of central Honshu island
